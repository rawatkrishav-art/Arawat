Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Is it free to list my vehicle? ▼
Yes! Listing your vehicle on VeloMart is completely free for 60 days. We charge zero commission on the sale. You keep 100% of your asking price.
How does VeloMart verify listings? ▼
Our team manually reviews every listing — checking photos, RC details, and seller contact info before it goes live. This keeps our marketplace genuine and trustworthy.
Can I negotiate the price with the seller? ▼
Absolutely. VeloMart connects you directly with the seller. Negotiation happens between buyer and seller — we don't interfere. Most sellers are open to reasonable offers.
How long does it take to sell a vehicle? ▼
Average deal time on VeloMart is under 24 hours for priced-right listings. Popular categories like bikes and compact cars typically sell within a few days.
What documents do I need for ownership transfer? ▼
You'll need the RC Book, insurance copy, PUC certificate, Form 29 & 30 (for transfer of ownership), and an NOC if the vehicle is financed. We provide a detailed checklist when your listing is approved.
